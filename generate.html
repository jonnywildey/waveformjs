How to add a new track

  1. Drop your MP3 here and download the two files
  2. Put the .svg in svg/stories/ and the .json in json/stories/
  3. Put the MP3 in audio/stories/
  4. Add an entry to the scData array at the top of player.js:
    { id: 'mytrack', url: 'audio/stories/mytrack.mp3', svgId: 'stories/mytrack.mp3', title: 'My Track' }
